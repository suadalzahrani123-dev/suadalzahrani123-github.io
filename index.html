About Sura

What makes Sura different?

Sura bridges travelers with local guides and “experience hosts” to live Saudi life from the inside — beyond big events and standard tours.

  • Classic tours: city highlights, museums, landmarks.
  • Seasonal practices: equestrian culture, calligraphy, heritage crafts, falconry and more.
  • Map-based matching: open the map and book the closest verified guide.

How it works (Traveler)

  1. Download the app (App Store / Google Play)
  2. Choose your city
  3. Browse local guides (bio, languages, ratings, price)
  4. Select a ready-made tour (city walk, museums, landmarks)
  5. Request & coordinate — sometimes pay in-app

How it works (Guide)

  1. Register as a Guide
  2. Create a profile
  3. Add public tours / experiences
  4. Receive booking requests
  5. Turn location On/Off and set service radius (5–20 km)

Help center (FAQ)

How do bookings work?
Choose a guide or experience, send a request, coordinate time, and confirm (pay in-app in the full product).
Is Sura a traditional tour app?
No. Sura emphasizes authentic local moments and heritage practices — beyond typical tours.
Refunds & cancellations
Demo policy: full refund 24h before start, partial refund within 24h. (To be defined with partners.)
Safety & verification
Demo policy: guide verification, clear meeting points, and post-experience reviews.
